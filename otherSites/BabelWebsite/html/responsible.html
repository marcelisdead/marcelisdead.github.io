Commitment to the Future

The Babel Company is focused on the future. We started this long before it was fashionable. For us, this is a calling and not just a business. A calling that we put our pioneering work, human ingenuity and whole bodies into-and have for generations.

Throughout our company's history, we have been guided by our founding principles and Our Credo-which compeled us to people.

Today, the world is a community. And our work is on that global stage.

We are proud to support the work and programs that we do.

Human First

Helping all of humanity requires people first. When all people, their families and thriving communities work together everything else will follow.

It is the responsibility of human beings to work with one another. We support the ones on the front lines at the heart of insuring our ability to address our greatest needs.

We don't do this alone. Through both global and community-based partnerships, we work with those in developing countries, women, men, babies and the next generation to develop what is needed to drive wellness one human at a time.

By galvanizing partners, connecting communities and leveraging our employees, volunteer, partners and extensive reach, we activate the resources-and resolve-needed to address the most pressing challenges.
