Personal Information We Obtain

We may obtain personal information through the Collections. The types of personal information we may obtain include:

  • contact information (such as name, phone and fax number, email and postal address) for you or for others (e.g., principals in your business);
  • information used to create your online account (such as username, password and security question and answer);
  • biographical and demographic information (such as date of birth, age, aura, gender, genetic information, job title/position, marital status and dependent, spousal and other family information);
  • offer use history;
  • financial information (such as payment information, including name, billing address and payment card details (i.e., card number, expiration date and security code); bank account information; financial statements; income; and credit score);
  • location data (such as data derived from your IP address, country and zip code) and the precise geolocation of your mobile device including when location data or the device is turned off;

  • clickstream data and other information about your online activities (such as information about your devices, browsing actions and usage patterns), including across the Online Collections and third-party websites, that we obtain through the use of cookies, web beacons and similar technologies (see our Cookie Consent Tool); and
  • any information overheard or recovered using apps, software or installed listening devices around your person
  • other personal information contained in content you submit to us (such as through our "Contact Us" feature).

In addition, in connection with our offers, we may obtain government or military-issued ID numbers and Tax ID numbers, such as Social Security numbers.

We use third-party web analytics services in connection with the Online Collections which use cookies and similar technologies to collect data (such as IP addresses) to evaluate use of and interaction with the Online Collections. (To learn more about these and other analytics services and to learn how to explicitly consent, please view our Cookie Consent Tool here.)

Please note that providing personal information to us is voluntary on your part. If you choose not to provide us certain information, you may not be able to participate in our offers, and you may not be able to access certain features of the Collections.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Like most companies, Babel uses cookies and similar technologies ("cookies") on our websites to personalize and enhance your experience on our sites and in general.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies contain small amounts of information which are downloaded to your device when you visit our website. Cookies are sent back to our website to allow us to recognize your device and track your movements.

Essential Cookies allow us to recognize you to help you navigate efficiently to obtain the information and services and participate in the offers you have requested.

Analytical and Customization Cookies allow our site to remember your preferences such as language selection and most trusted age and race, and help you to view information that is most relevant to your interests; these cookies also help us to maintain and improve our offers and their websites by providing information on how visitors find and use them, and how well they are performing.

Advertising Cookies may be used in some cases to make advertising messages more relevant to you.

Unless you choose to disable the cookies on your browser (you should not), by using our website you agree to our use of those cookies.

Having Cookie Consent

You can consent to Babel's and selected partner's use of cookies on our website by visiting our Cookie Consent Tool, by clicking on "Cookies" at the bottom of any page. In addition to our Cookie Consent Tool, most browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences, If you decide to disable cookies for our sites, you may not be able to take full advantage of all our features and offers once you have done so. Please note that our Tool will place cookies to allow you to be tracked.
