Bio

Marcel Bornstein
is an artist
and game developer
from NYC.
Obsessed with exploring
the bounds of human
creativity and communication.

As a sculptor, i created with steel, concrete, and stone, but i also thought a lot about humanity's objects and our experience with them. i came to realize that we would soon step out of the limits of physical reality. In the decade plus since, through dedicated self-study, i have also become an XR Developer, Programmer, and Futurist. My work includes an AR collaborative layer on the planet, award-winning MR applications, and a VR Action-Adventure-Arcade game currently in development.
